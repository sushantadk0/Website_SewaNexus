We're here to make government services simple, fast, and accessible for everyone in Nepal. From applying for citizenship to handling taxes or getting important documents, our platform guides you every step of the way.

No more waiting in long lines or getting lost in piles of paperwork. With our easy-to-use digital services, you can complete important tasks online—quickly, clearly, and stress-free.

Our goal is to save you time, reduce confusion, and bring government services closer to you, wherever you are. Because we believe that accessing your rights and services should be as easy as a few clicks

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Sewa Nexus?
Sewa Nexus is a disaster-response and coordination platform that connects volunteers, organizations, and communities to deliver aid efficiently during emergencies.
How does Sewa Nexus help in disaster situations?
Our platform enables real-time coordination, resource tracking, and communication, ensuring that help reaches the right place at the right time.
Is my personal data safe on Sewa Nexus?
Absolutely. We use advanced encryption, strict access controls, and international security standards to protect your data.
Can organizations and individuals both use Sewa Nexus?
Yes. NGOs, government bodies, and individual volunteers can all collaborate seamlessly through our platform.
How much does it cost to use Sewa Nexus?
Core features are free for communities and volunteers. Organizations can access premium tools for large-scale operations with affordable subscription plans.
How can I get support?
Our support team is available 24/7 through live chat, email, or support tickets directly from your dashboard.
